Governance

Out There Exeter is a Charitable Incorporated Organisation (CIO) that has been incorporated under the Charities Act 2006 and has its own legal identity and is governed accordingly. Out There Exeter's charity registration number is: XXXXXXXX

Governance Structure

Out There Exeter has put in place a governance structure that serves as the foundation upon which Out There Exeter operates. It defines the roles and responsibilities within the charity, establishing a framework for decision-making and strategic direction. Ultimately, the Trustees bear the responsibility for ensuring that Out There Exeter adheres to this framework and upholds its commitment to its mission, purpose, and goals. You can understand more about our structure by reading the charity constitution and by taking a look at our People page.

Decision-Making

The charity's decision-making process, outlined in the Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), fosters transparency, accountability, and gives clarity for beneficiaries, stakeholders, and regulators. Guided by the bylaws and the constitution, Trustees will make informed decisions collectively, grounded in evidence and good judgment.

Out There Exeter Constitution

The Out There Exeter constitution is a governing document, articles of association, or memorandum of association, that sets out the rules for running Out There Exeter. Our constitution helps to ensure that our organisation is run in a fair and transparent way, that protects the interests of our purpose and stakeholders.

Governance Code

Good governance is essential for the charity to achieve its goals and comply with laws and regulations. The charity is committed to the principles of the governance code endorsed by the Charity Commission, and it adheres to the Commission's Trustee Guidance, ensuring the highest standards of accountability and transparency.

Governance and Bylaw Documents

The bylaw documents are crucial components that define and guide the operation of Out There Exeter. These documents serve as the foundation for the charity's structure, objectives, and management practices, ensuring that Out There Exeter is run effectively and in accordance with its charitable purposes.

Standing Operating Procedures (SOPs)

Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) serve as the foundations of the charity's operations, ensuring consistency, quality, and adherence to its policies. SOPs will be reviewed and revised annually to ensure they remain effective, or as needed to reflect changes in the charity's strategies or the regulatory environment. Failure to comply with SOPs may result in investigation and disciplinary action, up to and including termination of role or restricted involvement with the charity.
