Queer Culture

Queer culture encompasses the diverse and vibrant experiences of LGBTQIA+ individuals. It's a tapestry woven from threads of self-expression, creativity, community, activism, and resilience.

Out There Exeter recognise the beauty and significance of queer culture to the LGBTQIA+ community. That's why we have created Queer Fest; a celebration but an opportunity to challenging the status quo and paving the way for a more inclusive and equitable world.

Come help us build tribes, and be part of the vibrant kaleidoscope of the queer culture that is Queer Fest .
