Charity Next Steps

Your invaluable feedback has played a pivotal role in shaping the foundations of the charity. We are currently dedicating time to crafting comprehensive policies and strategies, which we will present for your consideration at an upcoming general meeting.

Our journey towards becoming a registered Charitable Incorporated Organisation (CIO) with the Charity Commission is nearing completion. We have crafted our charity governing document, agreed upon our charity's purpose, and submitted our application.

Our committee leads are in place, and we are establishing our committee groups. These individuals and groups will play a crucial role in fostering strategic partnerships, securing essential funding, and bringing our vision for the 2024 festival to life.

Our primary focus in the coming months will encompass:

  • Finalising our charity's governance framework
  • Develop branding
  • Create a marketing strategy and a comms plan
  • Embarking on fundraising efforts and planning for the September 2024 festival
  • Developing a comprehensive directory

We are committed to providing regular updates during general meetings as well as through email communication. If you have any additional input regarding updates, our next course of action, or are interested in joining our WhatsApp group or committee groups, kindly inform us by sending an email to info@out-there-exeter.com.
